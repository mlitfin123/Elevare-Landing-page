# Prep Files #04

This week felt different.

Not because anything dramatic happened, or because I suddenly woke up feeling like I looked a lot better.

But because for the first time this prep, I feel like I'm actually settling into the routine.

Fifteen weeks sounds like a long time, but it goes fast and suddenly you're 4 weeks out trying to play catch up.

This week felt like the point where prep stopped being something I was trying to do and started becoming part of my normal routine again.

## What Changed This Week?

The biggest difference this week was consistency.

For the first time this prep, I followed the plan almost exactly the way I wanted to.

Unlike last week, the cardio got done.

The meals were where they needed to be and I was within striking distance of my macros every day.

Training stayed strong and I felt good in the gym.

Just a bunch of good days stacked together and it was noticeable at the end of the week.

Cardio is still hard. I don't think I'll ever genuinely enjoy spending time on a stairmaster.

But it's starting to feel normal again, and I even squeezed a run in there to mix things up.

Either way instead of fighting and dreading it, I'm just doing it and I'm hoping it'll continue getting easier. It normally does until I'm reaching the end of prep.

One thing I noticed this week was that I suddenly had a strong urge to go out and enjoy myself over the weekend.

Part of me kept thinking that I still have plenty of time before the show, in the past I've had success waiting till about 10-12 weeks out to really start pushing and 15 weeks sounds like a long time.

But every time I started thinking that way, I reminded myself of the goals I set when prep started.

I also know I'm probably not starting from a position where I can afford to coast.

If anything, I'm a little behind after not taking training and dieting seriously for the past couple years.

Because of that, I decided not to make any exceptions and just stick to the plan.

Looking back, I'm glad I did.

The scale is moving.

I feel tighter.

And for the first time this prep, I feel like I'm actually getting into prep mode mentally.

## My Thoughts

This was probably my favorite week of prep so far.

Not because I dropped a huge amount of weight or started looking a lot better.

But because I finally started feeling locked in.

Every prep has a point where you stop negotiating with yourself.

You stop looking for shortcuts.

You stop wondering whether you're really ready to commit.

It's like a switch happens in your mind and you just start doing what needs to be done.

I feel like I'm getting close to that point.

There are definitely going to be harder weeks ahead and I know I won't be able to be perfect next week, but I'm preparing for it.

There will be a stronger switch probably around 12, 6, 4, and 2 weeks out that lets me know I really need to lock in harder and can't veer off from the plan at this point.

It happens just about every prep I do and I'm looking forward to it.

The routine is becoming normal again and I'm starting to notice subtle changes.

For the first time this prep, I feel like I'm moving in the right direction both physically and mentally.
