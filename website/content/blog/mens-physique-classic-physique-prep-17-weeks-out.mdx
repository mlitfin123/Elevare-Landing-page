## What Changed This Week?

The biggest thing I noticed this week was that I'm still not 100% following the diet.

It's difficult mentally to go from enjoying whatever I want to suddenly tracking everything and limiting myself. I'd say I was about 70% on track this week.

I do feel like I'm tightening up a little already, but that's probably more from cleaning up my diet and adding cardio than from any meaningful fat loss at this point.

At this point, I'm less interested in individual days and more interested in the overall weekly trend.

Some days were great. Some weren't.

Mentally, I feel like I'm starting to settle into prep mode, and overall I'm happy with how the first week went.

## My Thoughts

I've done enough preps now that this doesn't surprise me.

One thing I've learned is that prep requires a lot more patience than people want to admit.

It's easy to look in the mirror every day and convince yourself that something needs to change.

It's much harder to stay objective and trust the process.

For now, the focus remains the same:

* Execute the plan
* Track the data as closely as I can
* Get my mind right because the time to really push will come quickly
