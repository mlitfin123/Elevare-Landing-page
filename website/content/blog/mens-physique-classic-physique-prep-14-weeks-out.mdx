# Prep Files #05

This definitely wasn't my best week.

I missed a workout.

I also decided to take a social day over the weekend.

Other than making sure I still hit my protein, I pretty much put prep on pause for the day.

Honestly, I don't regret taking the day.

I do regret not planning for it better.

## What Changed This Week?

The biggest thing I noticed this week was... not much.

The scale was up a little.

StageLab didn't really notice any visual changes compared to last week either.

To be honest, I'm not surprised.

I didn't give myself much of a chance to make progress.

The good news is that I still felt strong every time I was in the gym.

My motivation is still there.

This week wasn't about losing motivation.

It was just one of those weeks where life got in the way a little more than I let it before.

Looking back, I know exactly where I can improve.

Most of it comes down to planning better.

Prep is a lot easier when the meals are already made and the cardio is already scheduled.

## My Thoughts

This week feels more like a reminder than a setback.

It's easy to think that because you're still 14 weeks out, one week doesn't really matter.

Maybe that's true by itself.

But a few weeks like this in a row?

That's how prep starts getting away from you.

I definitely feel like I'm in catch-up mode now.

Not because I'm panicking, but because I know I can do better.

I'm not questioning the plan.

I'm questioning my execution.

I've been through this process enough times that it's not enough to just show up anymore.

I still believe I'll be ready, but the overall goal is to improve from my previous shows.

I still think I have enough time to get things right.

But I also know I can't keep having weeks like this and expect everything to magically work itself out.

This week is about getting back to doing the little things right.
