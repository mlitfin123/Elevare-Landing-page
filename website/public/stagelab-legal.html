Paid Users

  • Who are the users that will use the paid subscriptions in the app? Physique athletes and their coaches.
  • Where can users purchase the subscriptions that can be accessed in the app? The user can purchase subscriptions in their profile screen.
  • What specific types of previously purchased subscriptions can a user access in the app? Athlete subscription and coach subscription.
  • What paid content, subscriptions, or features are unlocked within the app that do not use In-App Purchase? The athlete user unlocks in app weekly coach recommendations and AI photo comparisons with subscriptions, while a coach user unlocks additional athlete slots to track their physique athlete clients.
  • Can users purchase physical goods or services together with digital content in your app? No.
