Company

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Scope

This Policy applies to users of Elevare, including clients and independent fitness professionals ("Trainers"). By using the Services, you consent to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Identity: name, email, phone, account credentials
  • Profile: fitness goals, preferences, availability, location (if enabled)
  • Trainer data: certifications, experience, media, pricing
  • Communications: messages, support requests, reviews

B. Transaction & Payment Data

  • Purchases, bookings, session history
  • Payment tokens and metadata via third-party processors (e.g., Stripe)
  • We do not store full card numbers

C. Device & Usage Data

  • IP address, device identifiers, OS, app usage events, crash logs

D. Location Data (Optional)

  • Approximate or precise location if you enable it for matching

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate and provide the Services
  • Match clients with Trainers
  • Process payments, credits, refunds, and fraud prevention
  • Facilitate communications and notifications
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Maintain safety, enforce policies, and resolve disputes
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Bases (if applicable)

Where required (e.g., certain jurisdictions), we rely on:

  • Contract performance
  • Legitimate interests (service improvement, safety)
  • Consent (e.g., location, marketing)
  • Legal compliance

5. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: hosting, analytics, payments (e.g., Stripe)

We do not sell personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution. Retention periods may vary by data type.
