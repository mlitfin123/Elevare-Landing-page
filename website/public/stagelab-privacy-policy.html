Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how StageLab collects, uses, stores, and shares information.

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how StageLab collects, uses, stores, and shares information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • name;
  • email address;
  • login credentials;
  • subscription status.

Fitness and Prep Data

  • bodyweight;
  • photos;
  • check-ins;
  • macros;
  • workouts;
  • cardio;
  • recovery data;
  • notes;
  • competition details.

Device and Usage Information

  • device identifiers;
  • IP address;
  • app interactions;
  • analytics.

Payment Information

Payments are processed by third-party providers.

StageLab does not store full payment card information.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • operate the Services;
  • generate AI recommendations;
  • provide coach workflows;
  • improve systems;
  • support subscriptions;
  • analyze performance;
  • provide customer support.

4. AI Processing

User data may be processed by AI systems to:

  • generate recommendations;
  • analyze visual progression;
  • estimate conditioning trends;
  • summarize prep status.

AI outputs may be imperfect and should not be considered medical advice.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • payment processors;
  • cloud hosting providers;
  • analytics providers;
  • AI service providers;
  • authorized coaches connected to athlete accounts.

We do not sell personal information.

6. Photo and Visual Data

Progress photos may be stored and processed to support:

  • visual comparisons;
  • AI analysis;
  • prep tracking;
  • historical review.

Private photos are stored in protected systems and are only accessible according to platform permissions.

7. Data Retention

We retain information as necessary to:

  • operate the Services;
  • preserve prep history;
  • comply with legal obligations.

8. User Rights

Users may request:

  • access to data;
  • correction of data;
  • deletion requests;
  • account closure.

9. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children

The Services are not intended for children under 18.

11. International Users

You understand information may be processed in the United States.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time.
