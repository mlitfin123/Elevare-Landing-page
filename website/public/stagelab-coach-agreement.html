Coach Agreement

This Coach Agreement governs participation by coaches using StageLab.

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

1. Independent Status

Coaches are independent users and not employees or representatives of StageLab.

2. Coach Responsibilities

Coaches are solely responsible for:

  • athlete guidance;
  • recommendations;
  • communications;
  • professional conduct.

3. No Medical Services

Coaches agree not to represent StageLab as a medical provider.

4. Athlete Data

Coaches may only access athlete information for authorized athlete relationships.

5. Prohibited Conduct

Coaches may not:

  • misuse athlete data;
  • harass athletes;
  • violate laws;
  • misrepresent credentials.

6. AI Assistance

AI outputs are assistive only.

Coaches remain responsible for final recommendations.

7. Termination

StageLab may suspend or terminate coach access at any time.
